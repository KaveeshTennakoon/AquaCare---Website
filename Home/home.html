AQUACARE

Our mission is to protect and restore marine biodiversity, combat pollution, promote sustainable fisheries, conserve habitats, address climate change, empower communities, educate younger generation, raise awareness, promote responsible tourism, encourage innovation and research, and foster collaboration for lasting impact. Through these efforts, we envision a future where marine ecosystems thrive, supporting ecological integrity, human well-being, and sustainable development
